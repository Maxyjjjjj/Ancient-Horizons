"Name: Snow Leopard\nScientific: Panthera uncia\nPeriod: Modern\nDescription:\nThe snow leopard is a magnificent big cat adapted to life in harsh mountain environments.\nIts thick fur and long tail help it survive in cold climates.\nFound in snowy mountain biomes, snow leopards are agile hunters capable of taking down prey much larger than themselves.\nThey can be tamed with meat. Tamed snow leopards can wear armor for protection.\nThese elusive cats are excellent climbers and can leap great distances across rocky terrain."
