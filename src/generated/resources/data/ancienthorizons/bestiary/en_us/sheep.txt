"Name: Sheep\nScientific: Ovis aries\nPeriod: Modern (Domesticated ~10,000 years ago)\nDescription:\nSheep are domesticated mammals raised primarily for wool, meat, and milk. Their wool can be dyed in many colors.\nFound in plains and mountain biomes, sheep graze on grass to regrow their wool. They can be bred using wheat.\nSheep can be sheared for 1-3 blocks of wool without harming them. They naturally spawn in white and sometimes in other colors.\nThese docile animals have been essential to human civilization, providing materials for clothing and textiles."
