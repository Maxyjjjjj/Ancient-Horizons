"Name: Crocodile\nScientific: Crocodylus niloticus\nPeriod: Modern (Ancient lineage 95 million years)\nDescription:\nCrocodiles are ancient reptiles that have remained largely unchanged for millions of years. They are ambush predators that lurk in water waiting for prey.\nThese reptiles cannot be tamed. They are most active during dawn and dusk.\nCrocodiles have the strongest bite force of any living animal and can hold their breath underwater for extended periods."
