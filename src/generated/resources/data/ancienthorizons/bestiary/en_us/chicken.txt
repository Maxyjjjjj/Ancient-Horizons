"Name: Chicken\nScientific: Gallus gallus domesticus\nPeriod: Modern (Domesticated ~6,000 years ago)\nDescription:\nChickens are domesticated birds raised for eggs and meat. They are the most common bird species in the world.\nFound in most biomes, chickens wander aimlessly and lay eggs periodically. They can be bred using seeds of any kind.\nChickens provide feathers and raw chicken when killed. Their eggs can be thrown to potentially spawn baby chicks.\nThese birds are descended from jungle fowl and have been bred into hundreds of varieties worldwide."
