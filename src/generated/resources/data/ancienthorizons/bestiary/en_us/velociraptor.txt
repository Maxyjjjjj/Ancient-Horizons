"Name: Velociraptor\nScientific: Velociraptor mongoliensis\nPeriod: Late Cretaceous (75-71 million years ago)\nDescription:\nVelociraptor was a small but intelligent theropod dinosaur known for its distinctive sickle-shaped claw on each foot. Despite popular depictions, they were actually turkey-sized and covered in feathers.\nThese pack hunters are highly aggressive and will attack in coordinated groups. They are found in desert and badlands biomes.\nVelociraptors can be tamed with raw meat, but require patience and skill. Once tamed, they become fierce protectors and excellent hunting companions.\nTheir intelligence and pack-hunting behavior made them formidable predators despite their small size."
