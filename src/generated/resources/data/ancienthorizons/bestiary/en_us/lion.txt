"Name: Lion\nScientific: Panthera leo\nPeriod: Modern\nDescription:\nThe lion is known as the king of beasts, famous for its majestic mane and powerful roar. Males have impressive manes while females do the majority of hunting.\nFound in savannas and grasslands, lions are social animals that live in prides. They are apex predators that hunt in coordinated groups.\nLions can be tamed with meat. Tamed lions are loyal protectors and formidable companions in battle.\nTheir roar can be heard from miles away and serves to establish territory and communicate with pride members."
