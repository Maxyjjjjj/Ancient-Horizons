"Name: Elephant\nScientific: Loxodonta africana\nPeriod: Modern\nDescription:\nElephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence, strong family bonds, and distinctive trunks.\nThese gentle giants roam savannas in herds, using their trunks to gather food and water. Despite their peaceful nature, they will charge when threatened.\nElephants can be tamed and used as mounts, capable of carrying multiple passengers. They have excellent memory and form deep bonds with their caretakers.\nTheir ivory tusks are valuable but should be protected, not harvested."
