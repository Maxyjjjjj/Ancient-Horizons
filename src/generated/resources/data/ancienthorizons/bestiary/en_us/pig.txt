"Name: Pig\nScientific: Sus scrofa domesticus\nPeriod: Modern (Domesticated ~9,000 years ago)\nDescription:\nPigs are intelligent domesticated animals raised primarily for meat. Despite their reputation, pigs are actually quite clean animals.\nFound in most overworld biomes, pigs are omnivores that will eat almost anything. They can be bred using carrots, potatoes, or beetroots.\nPigs can be ridden when equipped with a saddle and directed using a carrot on a stick.\nThese social animals are smarter than dogs and enjoy playing in mud to stay cool and protect their skin from parasites."
